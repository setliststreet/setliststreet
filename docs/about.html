About Setlist Street

The ultimate prediction game platform for Deadheads, created by fans for fans. Combining statistical analysis with community competition for the GD60 anniversary concerts.

♪

Our Mission

To enhance the live music experience by bringing Deadheads together through friendly competition, statistical insights, and community engagement. We celebrate the beautiful unpredictability of Dead & Company while finding patterns in the music we all love.

Platform Features

Real-Time Predictions

Make predictions before and during shows with live scoring updates

Statistical Insights

Access historical data and probability analytics for smarter predictions

Multiple Game Modes

From simple song predictions to complex setlist building challenges

Community Competition

Compete with fellow Deadheads on live leaderboards

Flexible Participation

Play for fun, charity, cash prizes, or sponsored rewards

Live Show Integration

Real-time next song predictions during actual performances

Fair Play Guaranteed

Transparent scoring, secure payments, and verified results
